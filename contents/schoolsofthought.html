Schools of Thought by Jacob - The Improviser

In a world where just about everything can be explained using science, we tend to disagree about things quite a bit. Every topic from politics, to philosophy and religion, and even science itself (to some degree) has some disagreement actively being disputed. This wouldn’t be so weird by itself, as no person can see the truth perfectly clearly, and thus different people have different perceptions of what the truth is especially when it comes to things that aren’t a predictable science.

However, this, very often, is not the case; most of the time, rather than arguments between people with a mostly similar understanding, most arguments happen between people on vastly different sides of an issue
